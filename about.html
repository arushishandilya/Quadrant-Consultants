About Quadrant

Quadrant Consultants is a growing organization in providing end to end Civil Engineering Management Consulting Services that form the basic building blocks required for implementing the Project Management.

We work closely with our valued customers to increase their readiness level to implement project in hassle free manner. This includes Complete Project Management Consultancy services, Detailed Project Report (DPR) preparation, Project Feasibility Reports, Design & Drawings preparation, Geotechnical investigations, Surveying, Risk Management, Cost effective implementation, Quality control, Inspection & Equipment management, Commissioning support, Pre-tender analysis & Agreements, Contracts & Claim handling, Techno-legal services, Insurance Claims Handling, Procurement Management, Liasoning, Valuation, and many more.

Who we are

Quadrant Consultants is a fast-growing organization offering end-to-end Civil Engineering and Project Management Consultancy. We specialize in DPR preparation, feasibility studies, design, surveying, risk management, cost-effective implementation, quality control, contract handling, procurement, valuation, and more—ensuring seamless project execution from start to finish.
